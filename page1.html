SAVE THEM ALL

Who We Are:

Best Friends operates the nation's largest no-kill sanctuary for companion animals and is committed to saving the lives of homeless pets by working with shelters and passionate people like you. Together, we will bring the whole country to no-kill by 2025. Together, we will Save Them All.

Vision:

A better world through kindness to animals.

Mission:

To bring about a time where there are No More Homeless Pets.

How to help/participate:

All around the country, cats and dogs who enter shelters are at risk of not making it out alive due to housing restrictions and other outdated laws in our communities. How can help change that? By teaming up with fellow community members and encouraging elected officials to support pet-friendly laws and policies. We're focusing on helping pets most in need by promoting pet-friendly housing, protecting outdoor cats in the community, ending breed restrictions, and eliminating puppy mills.
